Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Laylays Kitchen ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Laylays Kitchen is a food and recipe app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any data entered into the app (recipes, notes, etc.)

2. Local Storage

Any content you create or save within the App (such as recipes or preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data to begin with.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). Since we collect no personal information from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
