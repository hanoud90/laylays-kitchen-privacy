Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save a recipe?

Tap the save icon on any recipe to add it to your saved collection.

Is my data backed up?

All your recipes and preferences are stored locally on your device. We recommend keeping iCloud Backup enabled to avoid data loss.

How do I delete the app?

Press and hold the app icon on your home screen, tap "Remove App", then "Delete App".
